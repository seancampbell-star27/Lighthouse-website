Search

Manage and expand on your brand visibility across Google and Microsoft
(Bing). Reach existing and potential customers with optimized messaging and
strategic keyword bidding to drive consideration, intent, and conversion.
Employ Shopping and Performance Max opportunities to elevate your search
presence and eComm goals.

Includes:


Audit of past campaigns and historical performance
Strategic plan designed around your site experience, primary KPI, and
overall marketing goals
Account structure, including ad copy, asset management, and keyword
research
Management of vertical-specific policy needs, account verifications,
and platform specifics


Social Media

Amplify your existing presence on social media with strategically targeted
ads of all formats. Target existing followers and design lookalike
audiences based on their social-specific behaviors. At Lighthouse we
help you:


Identify the best platform(s) and audience targeting to achieve your
post-click goals
Align messaging with existing social presence for a cohesive ad
experience
Incorporate sound and movement and maximize all available ad formats
Stay up to date with policy updates, platform changes, and new
opportunities for your brand
Ensure brand safety using extensive knowledge of platform specifics


Video

Video is rapidly becoming an essential component to digital strategy,
reaching across channels, devices, and mediums. Engage your audience with
compelling movement and messaging, driving to your website via a click or
a QR code. At Lighthouse we'll present you with:


Cost-effective video opportunities in pre-roll, traditional TV, CTV or
OTT for your audience and geo
High-impact options to elevate your messaging, improve recall and drive
site traffic from TV
Attribution and retargeting options to maximize the impact of TV across
other channels
Detailed performance on content types, providers, and audience engagement

Programmatic

Access Display, Audio, Video, and Out-of-Home inventory via best-in-class
demand platforms. Make multiple channels work together to drive prospects
down funnel, build retargeting pools, and create demand. At Lighthouse
we offer:


Extensive programmatic buying experience within Basis DSP, Google, and
leading demand-side platforms
Inventory and tactic selection tailored to your available assets,
audience, and goals
Hyperfocus on brand safety and inventory quality, using a combination of
block-listing, exchange and domain-level optimization, and publisher
selection


Ad Operations

Effective optimization of your campaigns and in-depth analysis of your
data, to help you make informed decisions for your brand. At Lighthouse
we pride ourselves on:


Establishing a learning agenda during the planning phase, to align on
test-and-learn opportunities
Focusing optimization strategy around a primary KPI, while incorporating
secondary and macro-level goals
Providing consistent and relevant insights into your campaign
performance and audience engagement
