## Working media vs. fees

"Working media" is the portion of your budget that actually buys ad inventory.
Everything else — platform costs, agency management, ad-serving — should be
clearly separated so you always know your true media efficiency.

When those numbers are blended together, it becomes impossible to compare
channels fairly or to know whether a campaign is genuinely improving.

## What good reporting looks like

- Spend broken out by channel, with fees shown separately
- Pacing against budget, not just spend-to-date
- A primary KPI agreed in planning, reported consistently
- Caveats noted where attribution is correlational, not causal

## The takeaway

Transparency isn't a feature you bolt on at the end of a campaign. It's a
discipline that starts in planning and shows up in every report — and it's the
foundation of a media partnership you can actually trust.
